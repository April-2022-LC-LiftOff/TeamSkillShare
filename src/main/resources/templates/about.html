About

When our team got together to discuss making an application, we noticed that we all had the similar goal of wanting to create a way for people to share knowledge with one another. Through talking, we determined that it would be a community-focused knowledge sharing website. Users would be able to share what they know, and learn from others in their area. Our team was inspired to create something that would strengthen bonds within a community through sharing knowledge with one another, and (Skillshare) was born!
